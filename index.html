MY DRIVE

As a compassionate female student who truly cares about the well-being of others, I'm running for Welfare Secretary to be a voice and a support system for every student. I believe no one should be left behind whether it's in times of need, stress, or struggle.

With empathy, action, and dedication, I want to serve and ensure that our welfare is not just a concern, but a priority. I'm here to listen, to support, and to stand with you always. This is more than a position for me it is a responsibility I am ready to embrace with an open heart and a determined spirit.
